Washington D.C. Cherry Blossom Festival

About the Cherry Blossoms

The National Cherry Blossom Festival in Washington D.C. celebrates the gift of 3,000 cherry trees from Mayor Yukio Ozaki of Tokyo to the city in 1912. Every spring, visitors from around the world come to witness the beautiful cherry blossoms in full bloom.
